## Taxes on veterinary activities

- o Description : VAT charged on services provided by veterinarians.
- o Law : VAT Act, 2014.
- o Rate : Standard rate of 18%.
- o Example : Veterinary consultation fee charged at TZS 100,000. VAT calculation: 18% of TZS 100,000 = TZS 18,000. Total amount payable: TZS 118,000.

- o Rate : Varies depending on the specific drug (e.g., 10%, 25%).
- o Example : Veterinary drug imported with a customs value of TZS 1,000,000 and duty rate of 10%. Duty calculation: 10% of TZS 1,000,000 = TZS 100,000.

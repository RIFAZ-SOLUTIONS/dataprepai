## Taxes on Extraterritorial organizations and bodies operating

In Tanzania, such as embassies, international organizations, and NGOs, often have unique tax obligations, exemptions, and benefits compared to other business sectors. However, they may still be subject to certain local taxes, levies, duties, and customs under Tanzanian law. Below is a comprehensive list of 30 such obligations, including descriptions, applicable laws, rates, and calculation examples:

## 1. Value Added Tax (VAT)

- o Description : Tax on the value added to goods and services.
- o Law : Value Added Tax Act, 2014.
- o Rate : 18%.
- o Example Calculation : If an extraterritorial body purchases supplies worth TZS 1,000,000:
-  VAT = 18% × TZS 1,000,000 = TZS 180,000.

## 2. Pay As You Earn (PAYE)

- o Description : Income tax withheld from salaries of employees.
- o Law : Income Tax Act, 2004.
- o Rate : Progressive rates up to 30%.
- o Example Calculation : For an employee earning TZS 3,000,000 monthly:
-  PAYE = 30% × (TZS 3,000,000 - TZS 720,000) = TZS 684,000.

## 3. Social Security Contributions

- o Description : Contributions to social security for employees.
- o Law : National Social Security Fund Act, 1997.
- o Rate : 20% of payroll (employer and employee combined).
- o Example Calculation : For a monthly payroll of TZS 5,000,000:
-  Contribution = 20% × TZS 5,000,000 = TZS 1,000,000.

## 4. Skills Development Levy (SDL)

- o Description : Levy for vocational training.
- o Law : Vocational Education and Training Act, 1994.
- o Rate : 4% of payroll.
- o Example Calculation : For a monthly payroll of TZS 5,000,000:
-  SDL = 4% × TZS 5,000,000 = TZS 200,000.

## 5. Workers Compensation Fund (WCF) Contribution

- o Rate : 1% of payroll.
- o Example Calculation : For a monthly payroll of TZS 5,000,000:

## 6. Stamp Duty

- o Description : Tax on legal documents.
- o Law : Stamp Duty Act, 1972.
- o Rate : Typically 1% of contract value.
- o Example Calculation : If a lease agreement is valued at TZS 50,000,000:
-  Stamp Duty = 1% × TZS 50,000,000 = TZS 500,000.

## 7. Withholding Tax on Services

- o Description : Tax on payments to non-residents for services.

- o Rate : 15%.

## 8. Corporate Income Tax
